**House Sunderly of Saltcliffe** is a noble house from The Iron Islands. Their seat is located on the island of Saltcliffe. The Sunderly sigil is a drowned man, pink and pale, floating upright in a blue-green sea, his hair streaming upwards, as fish nibble at his limbs.

### Features

- **Castle** is the seat of House Sunderly
- **Village** consists of fishermen houses and small quarry
- **Ships** are anchored at wooden dock near the castle. Two of them are fishing boats and one is a longship.
