**House Stonehouse of Stonehouse** is a noble house from Old Wyk, one of The Iron Islands. Their sigil is a black brazier on a grey masonry field. Stonehouses are one of the most powerful houses sworn to House Greyjoy of Pyke.

### Features

* **The keep**, is a tall keep with few stone towers. It is located close to the sea on island of Great Wyk.
* **The village**, consists of few fishermen houses.
* **Port**, is where **The Red Jester** - longship of House Stonehouse, is anchored.

### Construction

This project was lead by KanylKay. Stonehouse was started in October of 2013. Keep was inspired by northern Scotland castles.
