**House Saltcliffe of Saltcliffe** is a noble house from The Iron Islands. Their lands and keep are located on the isle of Saltcliffe. Their blazon is a nine-headed serpent, black on silver. Their words are not mentioned in the books. Lord Donnor Saltcliffe is head of the house.

### Features

* **Castle**, is a grey stone keep with square towers.
* **Village**, consists of few fishermen houses
* **Longships**, three Saltcliffe ships (including **Hydra's Fury** and **The Serpent's Kiss**) were placed on the shore while **Seven Skulls** is sailing on the sea.
