**Description**  
  
House Hamell is a noble family from the Westerlands. The only lore about them is that a banner of House Hamell was among those gathered to escort the corpse of Tywin Lannister back to Casterly Rock.  
The lands of house Hamell are located at the border of the Westerlands and the Reach, south of Silverhall.
