# Introduction

Welcome to the WesterosCraft Wiki! **WesterosCraft** is a Minecraft server dedicated to recreating the continent of Westeros.
Westeros is part of the fictional world from the book series A Song of Ice and Fire, which was adapted into a TV show by HBO called Game of Thrones.
In this Wiki you will find Information about our Projects, Rules, Guides and Tutorials. For the latest updates head to our homepage.
Join, explore, and enjoy!
