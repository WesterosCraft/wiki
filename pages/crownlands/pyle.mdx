**Description**  
  
**House Pyle** is a noble house from the Crownlands. According to semi-canon sources they blazon their arms with a grey iron greathelm on white.  
Coat of Arms: A grey iron greathelm on white (Argent, an iron greathelm cendr√©e)  
Historical Members: Ser Howard Pyle, attended the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.  
The house is located north of KL, west of Byrch and south of Gaunt.  
  
**Features**  
  
Castle Pyle is a norman style keep with a curtain wall and 13 mural towers to defend the inner buildings and big open yard.  
Outside the castle is a small village with a sept and wind mills. The village is surrounded by several farms, including horse breeding facilities and an apple orchard.  
  
**Inspiration**  
  
The castle Pyle was mostly based off Framlingham Castle in Suffolk. The architecture of the houses was inspired by the surrounding lands of Gaunt and Mallery.
